fifield westerly

To add vibrancy and authentically capture the unity of multiple cultures that inhabit the River West neighborhood in Chicago,  Fifield Companies tapped Chicago-based street artist Sentrock to activate this truly incredibly at their brand new Westerly development.

Shake Shack

Shake Shack embraced the street art culture of the Old Fourth Ward neighborhood by commissioning Atlanta mural artist David Fratu. Known for his geometric and abstract art, David added a vibrant and organic piece to the space. The boldness of the mural surrounds their menu boards and compliments the Shake Shack aesthetic perfectly.

Learn More about our Shake Shack mural project

Chicago Fire

With the launch of its new logo and Stand for Chicago campaign, the Chicago Fire was looking for a bold statement to showcase its commitment to the community. By partnering with artist Max Sansing they created a mural to highlight and inspire the next generation of Chicago athletes.

Learn More about our Chicago Fire mural project

Square Payments

Square wanted to reach and engage small business owners through art. Using inspirational quotes and murals that showcased local Square users, this campaign was a unique way to demonstrate their support for the Atlanta business community.

Brookfield Properties
